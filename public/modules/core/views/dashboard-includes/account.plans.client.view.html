Merchant Plans

WHAT IS A MODERATED PAYMENT?
A Moderated Payment is used by merchants on a Business plan that have not been ID verified, and merchants on a Business Plus plan that have been ID verified and want to go above and beyond in establishing trust with the buyer. When a buyer purchases an item using the Moderated Payment method, the buyer is requested to make the payment into the multi-signature address. The merchant can verify that the payment is secured in the multi-signature account and provides the goods to the buyer. The payment is locked for the duration of the trade, until two of the three parties buyer, merchant and moderator agree to release it. There are two potential outcomes: When everything goes well, the buyer & merchant agree and release the payment to the control of the merchant. No intervention by the moderator is required in this case. When a dispute arises, the moderator can intervene, review the case and decide which party to side with either refunding the payment to the buyer, or releasing it to the merchant.
WHAT IS A BITCOIN DIRECT PAYMENT?
A Bitcoin Direct payment is offered by merchants on a Business Plus plan that have been ID verified. Funds are directed to the sellers digital wallet after a commission has been deducted. Payment is quick.
WHAT IS MERCHANT VERIFICATION?
Verification is activated after creating and connecting an ID verified BitPOS account to your trajans.market dashboard. Verified merchants display a 'Verified' badge on both their product pages and store front. Verification prevents fraud on the trajans.market platform and establishes a reputation of trust with potential buyers.
DO YOU HOLD USER FUNDS?
trajans.market never holds user funds. All funds are either locked in a 'multi signature account' or immediately directed to the merchants choice of digital wallet or BitPOS account after a sales commission has been deducted.
HOW DO I ACCEPT PAYMENTS?
Payment can be made by buyers in more than 20 digital currencies and are remitted to the merchant in bitcoin. Bitcoin funds are directed to the merchants digital wallet or BitPOS account. Merchants can easily enter their digital wallet address or BitPOS credential in the trajans.market dashboard.
WHO ARE BITPOS?
BitPOS has been serving the market since 2013, making them Australia's preferred, longest serving, and most experienced Bitcoin and Blockchain solution provider. Bitcoin remittance can be settled in AUD direct to a merchants bank account for a low 1% fee (deducted by BitPOS) or a combination of Bitcoin and AUD can be paid out depending on the merchants predefined preferences.
