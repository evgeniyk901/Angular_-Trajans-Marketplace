What is CoinJar Checkout?

CoinJar Checkout automatically converts the bitcoin your customers pay via Kryptonaut into Australian dollats and provides the means to deposit these funds direct into your Australian bank account. Coinjars' tools eliminate the risks of market fluctuation, yet still allows your customers to pay with bitcoin. The following, is a four step guide to create and link a CoinJar Merchant Profile with your Kryptonaut Merchant Account.

  1. Sign-up with CoinJar
  2. Setting up your CoinJar Merchant Profile
  3. Adding your Bank Account
  4. Integrate with your Kryptonaut Merchant Account and start selling

Sign-up with CoinJar

1. Visit the CoinJar website and choose 'Open a CoinJar today.' to create your CoinJar.

2. After filling out your details, you'll be sent an email with an activation link.

3. Follow your activation link to sign in to your CoinJar and select your CoinJar user name


Setting up your CoinJar Merchant Profile

Before you can convert the bitcoin your customers pay via Kryptonaut into local currency, you'll need to enter a few details into your CoinJar Merchant Profile. To get started, you'll first need to sign up to CoinJar.

1. Navigate to Checkout to begin. When you first load Checkout, you'll land on the Dashboard. Choose 'Get Started'.

2. Enter your contact information. You'll need to provide your business name, business address, country, and contact number. You must also enter in a support email.

Adding your Bank Account

When your customer pays you in bitcoin via Kryptonaut, those bitcoin are automatically converted into Australian Dollars for you. These funds can remain in your Checkout balance until you create a new transfer or can be automatically settled into your verified Australian bank account.

1. Navigate to Add a Bank Account

2. Fill out your account holder details. You'll need to provide the account name and your business address.

3. Enter your bank account information. You must provide your Australian bank account's routing code, account number, bank name, bank country and bank currency.

4. Choose 'Create Bank Account' to save your bank.
